Hero Details

Yve - Mage

Skill 1 - Void Blast

Yve sends a magic shockwave forward, dealing magic damage to enemies in its path.

Skill 2 - Void Crystal

Yve creates a gravitational field that pulls enemies towards a central point, dealing damage.

Ultimate - Real World Manipulation

Yve opens a large portal, damaging all enemies in the area and amplifying her magic damage.

Khufra - Tank

Skill 1 - Tyrant's Revenge

Khufra turns into a ball, rolling towards enemies, stunning and dealing damage to them.

Skill 2 - Bouncing Ball

Khufra launches an area attack, knocking enemies up and dealing damage.

Ultimate - Tyrant's Rage

Khufra pulls enemies towards him and deals damage, stunning them in the process.

Lesley - Marksman

Skill 1 - Master of Camouflage

Lesley camouflages herself, becoming invisible and gaining increased movement speed.

Skill 2 - Tactical Granade

Lesley locks onto an enemy and fires a powerful shot, dealing critical damage.

Ultimate - Ultimate Snipe

Lesley fires a long-range bullet that deals significant damage to an enemy hero.

Paquito - Fighter

Skill 1 - Heavy Left Punch

Paquito unleashes a series of punches, dealing damage to enemies in front of him.

Skill 2 - Jab

Paquito dashes forward, dealing damage to enemies and kthrows a jab.

Ultimate - Knockout Strike

Paquito launches an elbow strike in the target direction and knocking them back.

Hayabusa - Assassin

Skill 1 - Phantom Shuriken

Hayabusa throws three Shurikens in the target direction.

Skill 2 - Quad Shadow

Hayabusa dashes to the target, summoning multiple shadows to deal damage to seperate directions.

Ultimate - Ougi: Shadow Kill

Hayabusa blends into the shadows and launches single target attacks on the enemies.
