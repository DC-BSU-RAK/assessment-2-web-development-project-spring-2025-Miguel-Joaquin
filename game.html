What is Mobile Legends: Bang Bang?

Mobile Legends: Bang Bang (MLBB) is an action-packed 5v5 MOBA game created by Moonton. Created for mobile platforms, MLBB has enticed millions of gamers globally with its easy-to-learn mechanics and intricate strategic components

Basic Gameplay

  • 5v5 Battles: Two teams of five players face off to destroy each other's main base.
  • Three Lanes: Navigate bottom, middle, and top lanes while defending your turrets.
  • Lanes information Bottom/top can be either gold or exp lane while mid lane stays as a jungler
  • Jungle Area: Gain buffs and gold by defeating neutral monsters.
  • Objectives: Taking turtles, lords, and turrets to gain strategic advantages of the game.

Game Modes

Mobile Legends offers a variety of game modes:

  • Classic: Standard mode to practice new heroes with no rank consequences.
  • Ranked: Competitive gameplay with tiers and receive a rewards every end of the season.
  • Brawl: Fast-paced match with a single-lane battles.
  • Magic Chess: The game follows a strict gameplay wherein you start with a few heroes and gradually unlock more through rounds.
  • Custom: Create your own matches with friends.
